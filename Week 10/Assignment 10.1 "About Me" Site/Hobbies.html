Video Games!

I'm sure it comes as no surprise that an aspiring software developer enjoys video games! As someone who grew up loving fantasy and magic, I tend to gavitate towards role-playing/adventure games set in a fantastical world. Below are some of my favorite games.

  • Golden Sun: the Lost Age

    • Classic JRPG where teenagers tasked with stopping some impending doom explore an ancient world using their magical abilities
  • Kingdom Hearts 2

    • Because what more could you want than to conquer evil with a giant key and the friends you made along the way
  • Final Fantasy X

    • Young woman travels to offer her life in order to ensure that her world might know even just 10 years of peace

TV Shows!

From japanese anime, mindless reality drama, historical fiction, and everything in between, I love the art of television. I love seeing people share their vision and stories through so many different creative lenses.

  • The Legend of Korra

    • The follow up to another of my favorite shows, Avatar: the Last Airbender, follows the new avatar Korra as she strives to maintain balance in the world

    • Young, traumatized teenage kids tasked with piloting titanic creatures to save humanity? What could possibly go wrong :)
  • Rupaul's Drag Race

    • A group of talented drag artists must act, sew, sing, dance, and entertain in this reality competition for a crown and $100,000
